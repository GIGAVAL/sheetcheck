SheetCheck

Automated QA/QC for architectural drawing sets

SheetCheck reads the sheet index and every title block straight out of a drawing-set PDF, then cross-checks them against each other. It catches the quiet mistakes that slip into bid sets, missing sheets, unindexed pages, mis-titled drawings, and sheets bound out of order, before they turn into RFIs and addenda.
